EXAM GUIDE FOR STUDENTS

  1. first login into user portal with the login credentials sent on your register email address
  2. after successful login, click enroll exam to enroll for exam you are interested
  3. after enrollment, click exam date tab to view exam schedule in which you enrolled
  4. after viewing exam schedule, click start exam
    here the exam instructions and today's schedule are mentioned and click button to start exam
  5. after giving exam, click finish button to submit the exam
  6. click on the dashboard to view your score and also number of exams/courses you enrolled for
